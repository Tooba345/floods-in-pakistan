How You Can Help

Different ways to support flood-affected communities.

Donate

Financial contributions

Cash donations to trusted organizations allow them to respond flexibly â€“ providing food, clean water, medical supplies, shelter materials and support for rebuilding.

  • Use official websites and verified bank details.
  • Consider regular (monthly) donations for long-term recovery.
Volunteer

Volunteering your time

Volunteers help with relief distributions, data collection, health awareness, education support and community-based disaster-risk reduction.

  • Register with established NGOs or local authorities.
  • Take part in trainings on disaster safety and first aid.
Awareness

Spreading awareness

Sharing accurate information about flood risks, early warning messages and relief options helps communities prepare and respond more effectively.

  • Share information from official, trusted sources.
  • Support campaigns on climate justice and resilience.
Reminder: Always verify the legitimacy of any organization before donating or volunteering, and prioritise your own safety when visiting flood-affected areas.
